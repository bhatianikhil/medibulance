For bleeding cuts or wounds, here are some First Aid tips:
Stop Bleeding.
Apply direct pressure on the bleeding cut or wound with sterile gauze (if available) or a clean dry cloth, or tissue until bleeding stops.
Clean Cut or Wound.
Protect the Wound.
When to Seek Medical Help.
For Burns, here are some First Aid tips:
1. Stop Burning Immediately.
2. Remove Constrictive Clothing Immediately.
-For First-Degree Burns:
1. Cool Burn.
2. Protect Burn.
3. Treat Pain.
-For Second-Degree Burns:
1. Cool Burn.
2. Protect Burn.
3. Prevent Shock.
4. See a Doctor.
-For Third-Degree Burns:
1. Call 911.
2. Protect Burn Area.
3. Prevent Shock.
4. See a Doctor.
